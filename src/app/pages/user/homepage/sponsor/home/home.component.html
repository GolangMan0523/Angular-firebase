  • Form I-130 - Petition for Alien Relatives A citizen of the United States (1) may file Form I-130, Petition for Alien Relative, with U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) to establish the existence of a relationship to certain alien relatives (1) who wish to immigrate to the United States.
  • Form I-864 - Affidavit of Support Under Section 213A of the INA
    • This affidavit is required for most family-based immigrants (1) to show that they have adequate means of financial support and are not likely to become a public charge.
  • Form G-1145 - e-Notification of Application/Petition Acceptance(optional)
    • Used to request an electronic notification (e-Notification) when USCIS accepts 1's immigration application
