    • (optional) Used to collect additional information for a spouse beneficiary ({{us.data.application.name || "Applicant"}}) of Form I-130, Petition for Alien Relative.
  • Form DS-260 (Not Covered by Greenbroad)
    • DS-260 (Online Immigrant Visa and Alien Registration Application) needs to be submitted directly through Department of State's online portal, and is therefore not covered by Greenbroad. Later in the Submission 2 step, we will remind you to fill out this form on your own.
  • Form DS-5540 (Not Covered by Greenbroad)
    • Form DS-5540 is a 4-page form used to demonstrate that {{us.data.application.name || "Applicant"}} can be self-sufficient financially. {{us.data.application.name || "Applicant"}} will be asked to present a completed DS-5540 at the time of the visa interview. This form is not covered by Greenbroad. Later in the Next Steps section, we will remind you to fill out this form on your own.
