›What if I have changed my last name after getting married?

If you have legally changed your last name after getting married, you can use your new last name for this legal name question. On the next page of our software, we will ask you to enter any previous name(s) you have used and you can enter your old name(s) there. When you prepare the supporting documents later, if a document you provide shows a name that is different from your current legal name, you should provide along with that document a copy of a legal document reflecting the name change. As to what is considered a legal change of name document, USCIS Form I-130 Instructions Page 8 says "copies of the legal documents reflecting the name change, such as a marriage certificate, adoption decree, or court order." That list does include the marriage certificate.
