Step 4 of 5 - Submission 2 (Immigrant Visa Application)

Congratulations on advancing to Submission 2! In this submission, 0 will apply for an immigrant visa in order to travel to the U.S to receive a Green Card. Submission 2 will be handled by a new U.S. government agency called National Visa Center (NVC). It is part of the U.S. Department of State. Which forms does Greenbroad help you complete in Submission 2? You will need to submit two forms for Submission 2. One of the two forms (DS-260) needs to be submitted directly through Department of State's online portal, and is therefore not covered by Greenbroad. The other form (I-864) will be submitted separately and Greenbroad will help you with completing Form I-864. In addition to forms, you will need to submit two sets of documents (civil documents, and financial evidence) for Submission 2. Greenbroad will help you with collecting these documents. At the end of this step, we will also explain how to submit Submission 2.
