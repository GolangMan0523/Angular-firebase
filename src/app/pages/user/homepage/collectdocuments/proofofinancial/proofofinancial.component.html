Form I-485's evidence relating to public charge

Applicants are not required to provide any initial evidence relating to the public charge ground of inadmissibility with their Form I-485 (with one exception if the applicant received institutionalization). If USCIS requires additional evidence to determine if an applicant is inadmissible under the public charge ground of inadmissibility, it will issue a Request for Evidence and consider all evidence that the applicant provides in response.

Source: USCIS Form I-485 Instructions Page 16
